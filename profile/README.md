## About the company

**SRT — Smart Railway Technology S.r.l.** is a company of the **Salcef Group** that designs, builds, and maintains railway rolling stock and technologies. Operating on the global market, SRT covers the full spectrum of the railway industry — from track works and electrification to logistics, and the design, construction, sale, rental, and after-sales service of railway machinery.

With its operational headquarters in **Fano (PU), Italy**, SRT runs an Industry 4.0–oriented production hub where machinery and plants are interconnected into the company's information and logistics systems, enabling continuous production monitoring and KPI-driven decision making.

## Who we are

This organization is the **software and IoT engineering team** at SRT. We build the internal applications, management tools, and connected solutions that digitalize and optimize the company's industrial processes — turning the Industry 4.0 vision into the everyday tools used across our plants.

## What we do

- 🛠️ **Internal applications** — desktop and web tools for material coding, tracking, and management of production processes.
- 🌐 **IoT solutions** — interconnecting plants and machinery for real-time production monitoring and KPI analysis.
- 📊 **Data & integration** — pipelines and services that connect the company's information and logistics systems.
- ⚙️ **Automation** — CI/CD workflows and tooling to support the development team.

## On the horizon 🚀

This is where our work will grow. We are actively building — and planning to build — the next generation of internal engineering tools, including:

- **Codificatore SRT** — a desktop material-coding application set to replace legacy spreadsheet workflows with a structured, reliable database-driven tool.
- **IoT & telemetry platforms** — expanding real-time monitoring across plants and machinery.
- **Process automation services** — connecting design, production, and logistics into smoother end-to-end workflows.
- **Future internal tooling** — new projects will be added here as they take shape. This space reflects where SRT's engineering is headed.

> _More projects coming soon. Watch this space as we continue to build the digital future of railway technology._
